Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

1. Overview

Weaver ("we," "us," or "our") is a networking CRM designed for students. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our application at studentweaver.com.

By using Weaver, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account Information

When you sign up, we collect your email address to create and manage your account.

Contact and Networking Data

You may manually enter information about your professional contacts, including names, companies, roles, notes, follow-up dates, and call notes. This data is provided voluntarily by you.

Gmail Data

If you choose to connect your Gmail account, we access your email messages solely to help you track communication history with your networking contacts. We request only the minimum permissions needed for this functionality.

Google User Data Disclosure Weaver's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We do not use Google user data for advertising, and we do not sell this data to third parties.

Payment Information

If you subscribe to a paid plan, payment is processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number or full payment details on our servers. Stripe handles all payment information in accordance with PCI-DSS standards. We receive only a transaction confirmation and your subscription status.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Weaver service
  • Display your contacts, follow-ups, and communication history
  • Sync and display relevant emails from your connected Gmail account
  • Generate AI-powered summaries of your call notes
  • Process subscription payments through Stripe
  • Send you follow-up reminders and service notifications
  • Improve and develop new features

4. How We Use Gmail Data Specifically

When you connect Gmail, we access your email data only to match emails with contacts you have saved in Weaver. Specifically:

  • We read email metadata (sender, recipient, subject, date) and message content to display relevant communication history on your contact pages
  • We do not use your email content for advertising or marketing purposes
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your Gmail data to any third party
  • We do not use your Gmail data to train AI or machine learning models
  • We store only the data necessary to provide the email tracking feature, and you may disconnect Gmail at any time

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure authentication, and access controls to protect your information. While no system is 100% secure, we take reasonable precautions to safeguard your data.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Stripe — for processing payments
  • OpenAI — for generating AI-powered call note summaries (only the content you submit for summarization is sent)
  • Google — for Gmail integration (OAuth authentication only)

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your data at any time
  • Disconnect your Gmail account at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Request information about what data we store

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email listed below.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and maintain your session. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

Weaver is intended for college and university students. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of Weaver after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
